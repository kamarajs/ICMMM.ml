International Conference on Materials, Manufacturing and Machining (ICMMM 2.0) for Industry 4.0

About the Conference
In the present scenario, the role of Engineers/Researchers/Scientists is to enrich the societal development through green engineering for the health and wealth of human life. The objective of this conference is to enhance the knowledge in the integration of advanced materials, modem manufacturing, and machining processes to meet our requirements of Industry 4.0. The conference provides a technical forum for academicians, industrialists, and researchers for sharing knowledge, expertise, and experience.

Conference Theme - 'Industry 4.0'
Synchronized technical sessions will be conducted through virtual mode on the following themes to meet the requirement of industry 4.0

  • Smart/ Intelligent Materials
  • Innovative Manufacturing Technologies
  • Advanced Machining Techniques
  • Intelligent Technologies (AR. VR, Al, loT devices in Python)

ABOUT BIT

The Bannari Amman Institute of Technology was established in 1996 by the Bannari Amman Group, serves as one of the citadels of higher education in the Southern part of India. BIT aids to impart knowledge, teamwork, innovation, entrepreneurship, courage, sacrifice and duty which are innards of a meaningful life. It is an impressive campus situated in a serene surrounding at the foothills of the Nilgiris Mountains encompassing a sprawling area of 181 acres with a built-up area of more than 30 lakh square feet green campus and presenting an exemplary illustration of corporate contribution to society. The Institute has a team of 552 dedicated members of faculty, 167 with PhD and 225 pursuing PhD programmes. It offers 20 undergraduate programmes and 15 postgraduate programmes and PhD programmes in Engineering, Technology and Management. BIT also has 25 sophisticated research laboratories and 9 centres of excellence for the betterment of students, research scholars and faculty members. The Institution is approved by AICTE, New Delhi. All eligible courses have been ac-credited by National Board of Accreditation (NBA). The institution is re-accredited by National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) with a CGPA 3.57 on a four-point scale with 'A' Grade (2015). BIT has achieved 100% placement for the third consecutive year.

About Department

The Department of Mechanical Engineering is one of the elite departments of BIT functioning since 1996. The Department offers an Undergraduate Programme (B.E) in Mechanical Engineering and three Postgraduate Programmes (MI.) in Engineering Design, (M.E.) in CAD/CAM, and (M.E.) in Industrial Safety Engineering and PhD programmes.
